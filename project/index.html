Explore Nagaoka's Rich History

Step back in time and explore Nagaoka's fascinating history. Learn about its origins, its resilience in the face of adversity, and the vibrant culture that has shaped the city into what it is today.

Discover Nagaoka's Attractions

From the tranquil waters of the Shinano River to the breathtaking views from Mount Kakuda, Nagaoka is home to a wealth of natural beauty and attractions. Explore our parks, museums, and landmarks to experience the best of Nagaoka's natural and cultural heritage.

Experience Nagaoka's Festivals

Nagaoka is famous for its vibrant festivals, including the spectacular Nagaoka Fireworks Festival, one of Japan's largest fireworks displays. Experience the excitement and energy of our festivals, and immerse yourself in the rich traditions of Nagaoka.
